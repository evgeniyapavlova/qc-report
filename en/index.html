Q1

The first quarter laid the foundation for a successful start to 2020—trading volume on our platform was growing, as well as the number of active traders. The main growth and the largest volume of trade came from Turbo- and Digital Options; by region—in Asia and Latin America, with Brazil as the standout, continuing to lead in terms of trade volume.

March was marked by the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic, the financial crisis, and high volatility in the markets, which eventually brought the company record profits. Among instruments, Forex options were added to the previous leaders, and Europe also showed a noticeable increase from the regions. EBITDA projection at the end of the quarter was $142M.

Q2

The second quarter began successfully for the company. The prevailing market situation with its high volatility and the crisis in the market provided a large volume of trade and allowed maintaining income at the level of March. At the same time, marketing expenses increased, which became possible due to the improvement in the dynamics of return on marketing expenses.

After a slight decline in income in May, when there was a decline in trading activity, June was again one of the most successful months in the history of the company. The main growth came from Turbo options and Forex, while Latin America was again the growth leader in the regions. Q2 EBITDA projection increased by 18% to $168M.

Q3

July became the most profitable month in the company's history. The volume of trades on Digital Options and Forex was actively growing. As for regions, the main growth came from Latin America, Asia and Africa. August was marked by a slight drop in income. But there was positive news—we managed to agree on lowering withdrawal fees for clients in Latin America with Pay Retailers, one of the most important payment systems for us. September was quite successful compared to the previous month; trading activity increased, and the first results were noted on the reduction of costs for PSP-commissions. EBITDA projection for the quarter was $210M, up 25% from the previous quarter.

Verification of new users in the era COVID

Maxime François
Chief of Global Compliance and Back Office

Fast user verification is an important part of user satisfaction and their activity on the platform. We managed to maintain a high speed of verification despite the challenges of the COVID era.

What happened?

In March 2020, Jumio, our main provider of automated document verification KYC (Know Your Customer), was hit hard by the COVID crisis and closed some of its offices. They weren’t able to provide us with services at the required speed, which meant that we had to verify documents manually. By the end of March, the number of KYC documents awaiting verification soared to 8,000; by April we were able to reduce it to 4,000, and in May the number of documents rose again to 9,500, because the first wave of the pandemic brought us a lot of customers.

Number of clients waiting for verification

How did we resolve this?

Thanks to our Back Office team, by June we had reduced the number of documents to 1500, and in November the number of clients waiting for verification for more than 3 days approached zero. We’ve completely resolved the situation with Jumio and are working on adding a second service provider.

Creation of B2B business

Kirill Bolotov
Chief of B2B

Developing B2B means building a strong IT brand, entering a new market, new long-term clients, and new products that appeal not only to brokers but also to banks. We're developing three B2B areas: SaaS, Banking and Liquidity.

SaaS

Software as a Service is the provision of software as a service. In our case, we provide mobile, desktop and web versions of the trading platform to our clients: banks, prospective and current brokers and fintech companies.

What do you need to know about our SaaS solution?

Our platform has a 99.95% uptime.

We take care of the tech maintenance, so the customer doesn't have to waste their time on it.

Our platform is customisable, i.e., it is adjustable to the customer's requirements.

The applications we provide meet all the requirements of marketplaces.

Banking

In 2020, we started working on the banking infrastructure for both our group of companies and our B2B clients.

Why banking?

With the help of Amaiz, Westiva and Westiva Brazil banks, we'll be able to significantly reduce the company's bank fees and billing costs.

We provide B2C clients of our brokers with the ability to replenish the platform balance and withdraw funds via bank card.

Regulated and unregulated partners (traffic providers) will receive a convenient tool for withdrawing earned funds and reinvesting in advertising on our platform.

Liquidity

One of Quadcode's goals is to become not only a software developer, but also a liquidity provider for our customers. When Quadcode Markets receives its FCA licence, it'll be able to provide liquidity to all our group companies as well as all our B2B clients.

We'll do everything we can to make each B2B work well and the client experience as positive as possible.

We're increasing engagement

This is in line with the Data-driven approach: any decision must be justified by the impact on the company’s business objectives and backed up by numbers.

Launch of People Partners

People Partners is a new step in the development of the Case-managers team. The team was originally set up to deal with difficult situations, but gradually began to cover an ever-increasing pool of tasks. Now, as People Partners, we work not only with problems, but also with many very positive areas, such as coaching, identifying potential, or supporting promotions. The main objective of our team is to help the company achieve its business goals by building processes within and between teams. Our mission is to create a harmonious space for employees to reach their potential in the interests of the company.

Операционный HR

Tatyana Mikhaylova
HR Business Partner

В этом году мы сфокусировались на улучшении HR‑процессов и адаптации к новым реалиям этого мира для достижения наших бизнес‑целей.

Мы работали над разделением брендов нашего холдинга для рынка труда, формировали новые команды, занимались улучшением процессов и качеством сервиса нашего департамента, начали наш путь роста в Data‑driven направлении и сформировали новый взгляд для направления Talent Acquisition.

Разделение брендов

Привлекали таланты в новый и неизвестный на рынке труда бренд, сформировали внутреннюю политику по разделению брендов, создавали новые карьерные страницы, разрабатывали карьерный сайт для бренда Quadcode и помогли сформировать стратегию развития нашего бренда работодателя.

Улучшение качества сервиса

Запустили процесс найма под ключ и релокации в офис на Гибралтаре. На первой космической скорости адаптировали найм и внутренние процессы по адаптации, оформлению, приему, увольнению и даже релокации сотрудников в условиях удаленки. Сформировали политику удаленного найма сотрудников и наняли уже первых сотрудников на постоянную удаленную работу. Проводили глубинные интервью для анализа проблем среди наших заказчиков и сформировали много улучшений нашего Hiring Flow, которые продолжим интегрировать в наши процессы и в следующем году.

Рост в Data-driven направлении

Сформировали, научились считать и корректно вести данные для построения метрик в HR. Для увеличения скорости процессов и аналитики воронки найма переехали на новую базу данных для ведения кандидатов — Huntflow. На Кипре внедрили новое решение для ведения всех HR-процессов — BambooHR. Внедрили на регулярной основе аналитику заработных плат для Продуктовой и ИТ ветки. Вместе с командой аналитики получили рабочий инструмент для работы с парсингом данных с hh.ru. Взяли в работу оцифровку наших репортов по найму и заявки на найм в CIS. Автоматизировали больничный без больничного и внесли своими силами огромное количество доработок в 1С.

Новый взгляд для направления Talent Acquisition

Мы начали трансформацию для перехода в направление Talent Acquisition и разделение команды на 2 части: Sourcing и Recruiting. Такое разделение позволит членам команды сфокусироваться на каждом из направлений.

Corporate culture

Natalia Romashkina
Head of Corporate Culture

Our company has always paid a great deal of attention to corporate life. To make out-of-work activities even cooler, the Corporate Culture Department was created in October.

Now we've grown from a startup to a mature company. That's why our corporate culture is also changing. We have an important goal—to make events not only fun, but also beneficial for employees and the company.

Corporate culture council

To ensure that our corporate life goes hand in hand with the overall direction of the company and helps it achieve its goals, we started working together with the Strategic HR team. We make decisions together, with the help of the corporate culture council. There are three teams in the CCD: Sports, Events and Travel. And the council includes all those involved in the non-work activities in the company—Office Team, Strategic HR and soon representatives of all our small offices. The main idea of the council is to set the vector of corporate life and plan events not intuitively, but on the basis of data, and the task of Corporate Culture и Office Team is to implement on a high level what's planned by the council.
